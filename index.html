05 — Approach

How I architect
AI systems.

My process is deliberate, not reactive — from problem framing to deployed and monitored production systems.

01
🎯
PROBLEM FRAMING
Start with the business constraint, not the model. What breaks in production? What's the acceptable latency? What does "correct" actually mean?
→
02
🏗️
ARCHITECTURE DESIGN
Choose the right components — LLM, retrieval layer, vector store, agent tools — and design data flow with fallback handling before writing a line of code.
→
03
⚗️
BUILD & EVALUATE
Rapid iteration with rigorous evaluation at every stage. Benchmark retrieval quality, prompt outputs, and latency — not just final accuracy.
→
04
🚀
DEPLOY & MONITOR
Docker, MLflow, CI/CD. I treat AI systems as software products — deployed with logging, monitoring, and the ability to iterate post-launch without regression.
